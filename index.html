Services

HydrographicDipz specializes in adding that eye candy to just about anything with that out of the box finish. Call us for a free consultation on your next project!

Hydro Dipping

Car engine covers, brake calipers, rims, hard hats, controllers, cases, emblems, motor vehicle parts, and just about anything plastic or metal.

Fabricated Finishing

Our process adheres the patterns of your choice with a manufactured finish in your choice of matte or gloss.

Powder Coating - coming soon!

Due to popular demand, we are in the process of setting up a coating facility.
